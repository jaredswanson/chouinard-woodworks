Behind the Wood

For five decades, Rene Chouinard has been transforming the finest hardwoods into heirloom-quality pieces from his workshop in Winthrop by the sea. Each item – from intricately designed charcuterie boards to personalized step stools – reflects his unwavering commitment to quality and the joy he finds in his craft. Working with cherry, maple, oak, hickory, walnut, mahogany, purple heart, ebony, and other select woods, Rene creates pieces that are both functional and beautiful, designed to be cherished for generations.

FIND US

Located just 6 miles from downtown Boston in scenic Winthrop, Massachusetts, Chouinard Woodworks brings fine craftsmanship to the greater Boston area. Visit us at:

  • Winthrop Farmers Market: June through October
  • Local Craft Fairs: Check our events calendar for upcoming shows
  • Workshop Visits: Available by appointment only for custom orders

Convenient to the Orient Heights MBTA Blue Line stop and just over the bridge from East Boston
